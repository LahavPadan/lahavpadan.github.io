AUC-ROC

How ROC curves summarize threshold trade-offs, why AUC is an area, and why it also equals a positive-versus-negative ranking probability.

AUC as area

The ROC curve is evaluated at the distinct score values produced by the classifier. At one score level, every sample with that exact score enters together.

The empirical ROC curve used for AUC joins neighboring threshold points by straight segments. The area below one such segment is the rectangle below its lower endpoint plus the triangle created by its rise.
