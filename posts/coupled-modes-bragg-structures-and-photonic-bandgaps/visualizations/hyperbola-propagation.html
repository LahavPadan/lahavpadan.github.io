Why κ acts like a mass-like threshold

Write the relation as δ² = q² + κ². Before any real spatial wavenumber can appear, |δ| must first reach κ. This is the same threshold structure as the relativistic energy relation; the analogy is in the algebra, not in the physical origin of κ.
