§ 8.5 · Fresnel + kinematic phase

Two different phase histories, the same return phase

At the design wavelength, the first reflection acquires its half-cycle at the air|H interface. The next reflection acquires the same half-cycle from a round trip through the quarter-wave H layer.

φreturn = φFresnel + φprop  (mod 2π)
